The Dream

[12:4-5]

Yusuf said to his Father[Yaqoob], "Oh Father, I saw[in a dream] eleven stars and the Sun and the Moon, I saw them prostrating to me."

Yaqoob said to his Son[Yusuf], "Oh My Son, do not storytell your vision to your brothers for they may plan a plot against you,
Indeed Satan is a clear enemy to mankind."
