The Seduction

[12:23-25]

And she, in whose house he was, sought to seduce him. She closed the doors and said "You Come".
He said "It is Time, Allah is my Lord, he has made my stay excellent,
indeed he will not let the wrongdoers succeed."

She desired him and he would have certainly desired her if he had not seen the proof of his Lord.
Thus do we avert the lust and indecency from our excellent subjects.

And he raced to the door and she tore his shirt from the back, and she found her husband at the door;
She said, "What is penalty for someone who intended lust for your family/wife, except imprisonment or a painful punishment."
