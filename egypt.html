Egypt

[12:99-101]

Thus when they entered upon Yusuf, he welcomed his parents
and said "Enter Egypt, Allah willing, believing"

And he raised his parents to the throne, and they bowed to him in prostration,
and he said "O my Father, this is the interpretation of my past dream. My Lord has made it Truth. And he was certainly good to me when he released me from prison and brought you here from bedouin life after Satan had induced rivalry between me and my siblings.
Indeed my Lord is Subtle in what he wills, indeed, it is He who is the Knowing, the Wise.

My Lord, you have given me authority and taught me the interpretation of stories.
O originator of the Heavens and the Earth! You are my Guardian in this world and the End.
Cause me to die a Muslim and join me with the righteous."
