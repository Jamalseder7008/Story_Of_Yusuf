The Brothers

[12:63-66]

So when they returned to their father, they said, "O our father, they have restricted further supplies, so send with us our brother so that we may receive more and we will be protective of him."

...

He said, "Should I entrust you with him as I once entrusted you with his brother before? Thus Allah is the best protector and he is the Most Merciful of the merciful."

He said, "Never, will I send him with you
until you give me a oath by Allah that you will bring him to me
unless you are completely overpowered."
Once they had given him their oaths, he said, "Allah is mindful to what we have said."
