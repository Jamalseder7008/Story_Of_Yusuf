The Hidden

[12:102-105]

That is from the stories of the unseen which We reveal to you and you were not with them when they put together their plan.

And most of the people, even if you persist, will not believe.

And you do not ask them for a reward, indeed it is only a reminder to the worlds.

And of which of the signs in the Heavens and the Earth do they pass by with indifference.
