The Brothers

[12:8-10]

They said "Yusuf and his brother are more loved than us by our father and we are a strong group. Indeed our father is in clear error.

Kill Yusuf or cast him out to a distant land so that your father's attention will be yours and you will be after this a group of righteous people.

One of the speakers of them said, "Do not kill Yusuf and rather throw him in the bottom of the well so the caravan will pick him up."

Ask Yaqoob
