Brothers' Deceit

[12:16-18]

The Brothers returned to their father weeping, "Our father, we went racing and left Yusuf with our belongings, and a wolf ate him.
And you would not believe us even if we were truthful.

And they brought upon his shirt false blood. He said "No, your souls have enticed you to a command so patience is most fitting for us.
And Allah is the helper about that which you claim."

Yusuf and The Caravan
Go to sleep for several years
