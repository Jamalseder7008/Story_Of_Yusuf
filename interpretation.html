The Prison

[12:39-41]

"Oh my fellow prisoners! Which is better? Multiple lords or Allah, the One, the Prevailing?

You worship not besides Him except names you have named them, you and your fathers.
There is no proof or evidence that has been sent down regarding them.
Wisdom is for Allah. He has commanded that you worship not except Him.
That is the correct religion, but most of the people do not know."

"Oh fellow prisoners, one of you will serve wine to his master; but as for the last, he will be crucified and the birds will eat from his head."

One will go free
