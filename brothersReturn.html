Yusuf's Brother

[12:69-72]

And when they entered Yusuf's presence, he called his brother to his side.
He said "I am your brother so do not despair over what they used to do."

So when he had furnished them with their goods, he put the grail(cup) in his brother's bag
Then a caller from among them said "O people of the caravan, indeed you are thieves!"

They asked, turning around "What is it you are searching for?"

They said, "We are looking for the grail of the king, and he who finds it gets a camel's load of supplies. And I am responsible for it."

The brothers did not come here to steal
