The King's Service

[12:54-57]

And the King said "Bring him to me; I will appoint him exclusively for myself." And when the King spoke to Yusuf he said "Indeed, you are today established in a position and trusted."

Yusuf said "Declare me in charge of the storehouses of the land, I am protective and conscious."

And thus we established Yusuf in the land to settle wherever he willed. We touch with Our Mercy whom we will, and we do not allow to be lost the reward of those who do good.
And the reward of the Hereafter is better for those who believed and were conscious of their relationship with God.
