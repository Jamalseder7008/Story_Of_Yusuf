The Prison

[12:42-45]

And he said to the one whom he knew would go free "Remember me before your master", but Satan made him forget and he stayed in the prison for several years.

And the King said "I dreamt of seven fat cows that were being eaten by seven lean cows and seven bushels of greenery and others that were hard. O chiefs, explain to me my vision if you can interpret visions."

They said "[It is but] a mixture of distorted dreams and we are not knowledgeable of the interpretion of dreams."

And the one who was freed from the prison said "I will inform you of its interpretation, so send me forth."
