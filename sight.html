The Scent of Yusuf

[12:94-96]

And when the caravan departed [from Egypt],
their father said, "I sense the scent of Yusuf, if you do not find me deluded."

They replied, "By Allah! indeed you are in your old delusion."

Thus when the bearer of good tidings arrived, he cast it over his face so he regained his sight.
He said "Did I not say to you that I know from Allah that which you do not know?"
