Family Trial

[12:26-29]

Yusuf said "She attempted to seduce my soul"
and a witness from her family testified "If his shirt is torn from the front then she is truthful and he is a liar.
And if his shirt is torn from the back then she has lied and he is of the truthful."

So when the Husband saw Yusuf's shirt torn from the back, he said "This is from your plan, indeed your plan is evident.

Yusuf ignore this, and she must seek forgiveness for the sin. Indeed you were of the wrongdoers."

The Women of the city heard about the incident
Yusuf is imprisoned for his protection
