The Brother Stays

[12:77-81]

They said "If he steals, then his previous brother had stolen."

...

They said "Oh Leader, indeed he has a father who is an old man, so take one of us in place of him.
Indeed we see you as a doer of good."

Yusuf said "Allah has not chosen that we should take anyone except him, indeed we would then be unjust."

Return to your Father then say "O our father, indeed your son has stolen, and we do not testify except to what we knew.
And we could not protect against the unseen."
