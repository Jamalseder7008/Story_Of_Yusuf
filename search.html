Search for Yusuf and his Brother

[12:88-90]

So when they entered Yusuf's presence, they said, "O Leader, We and our family have been touched with hardship
And We have come with salvaged goods, please give us enough supplies and be charitable to us. Indeed Allah rewards the charitable."

He said "Do you know what you did with Yusuf and his brother in your ignorance?"

They said "Are you really Yusuf?!"
He said "I am Yusuf and this is my brother!
Allah has certainly been gracious to us. Surely whoever is mindful of Allah and is patient, then certainly Allah will not allow to be lost the reward of those who do good."
