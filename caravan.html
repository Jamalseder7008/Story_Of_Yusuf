The Caravan

[12:19-21]

And there came a caravan and the water boy reached into the well to bring up a sip of water;
He said "Oh Good News, There is a child" so they hid him as merchandise and Allah is more knowing of what they do.

They sold him for a reduced price.

The buyer from Egypt told his wife "Make his stay excellent, he may be a great use to us or we may take him as our own child."
