The Brothers Entrance

[12:58-61]

And Yusuf's brothers came and entered his presence.
He recognized them but they were unaware of who he really was.

And when he prepared their supplies, he said "Bring me your brother of yours from your father's side, do you not see that I give you enough and I am the best of accomadators?

But if you do not bring him to me, then I will not have enough[grain] for you, nor will you approach me."

They said "We will convince his father and we guarantee it."
