Conclusion

[12:109-111]

And we have not sent before them except men from the townships.
Do they not travel through the land to see what was the end of those before them?
And the Home of the End is better for those mindful [of Allah]. Thus will you not reason?

Until, when the messengers despaired and were certain they had been denied,
there came to them victory, and whoever We willed was saved.
And Our punishment cannot be averted by the wicked people

Verily in their stories there is a lesson for people of reason.
This story is not a fabrication and rather a confirmation of what has occurred
and a detailed explanation of All things
and guidance and mercy for people that believe.
