The Banquet

[12:30-31]

And the women in the city said, "The wife of al-Azeez is trying to seduce her slave boy; he has her in love.
Indeed we see her in clear delusion."

So when she heard them, she invited them to a banquet and she gave each of them a knife, and she told Yusuf "Come out before them."
So when they saw him, as they were admiring his beauty they cut their hands, and we said "Perfect is Allah, this is not a man; indeed this is none but a noble angel."

Yusuf must be imprisoned for his protection
