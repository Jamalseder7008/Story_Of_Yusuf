The Brothers return to their Father

[12:83-87]

Yaqoob said "No! Your souls have enticed you to something so patience is most fitting. Perhaps Allah will bring them to me all together.
Indeed it is He who is the All-Knowing, All-Wise."

They said, "By Allah, you will not cease remembering Yusuf until you become fatally ill or become of those who perish."

He said, "I only complain of my suffering and my grief to Allah, and I know from Allah that which you do not know.

O my sons! Go search for Yusuf and his brother, and do not despair of the spirit of Allah. Indeed no one loses hope in the spirit of Allah except the disbelieving group."
